# Part 4: The Glutton’s Appetite

NARRATOR:
When the door slammed open.

Rin stood in the doorway, her auburn hair wild, her tie now completely missing. Behind her, two demon students—the bull-horned boy and moth-antennaed girl from Seraphina’s entourage—looked distinctly uncomfortable.

“Found him!” Rin announced, pointing at Kaelen with theatrical flair. “See? Told you I could track my own marks.”

The bull-horned boy, whose name Kaelen had learned was Vorg, crossed his massive arms. “Lady Seraphina said to retrieve him. Not to barge into the human dormitory like a—”

“Like a what?” Rin’s eyes flashed. “Say it. I dare you.”

Vorg wisely said nothing.

Elara looked up from her book, her gaze flicking between Rin and Kaelen. “Is there a problem?”

“No problem!” Rin bounded into the room, completely ignoring personal space. She grabbed Kaelen’s arm, pressing herself against him in a way that made Cedric’s jaw drop. “I’m just taking my new toy for a walk. Lady Seraphina wants to have a family dinner with her dear cousin, and I’m the escort.”

Kaelen felt Rin’s mana thrumming against his skin, warm and hungry. “I wasn’t aware I had dinner plans.”

“You do now.” She tugged him toward the door, then paused, looking back at Elara with sudden interest. “Ooh, you smell interesting too. A human studying demon biology? That’s either very brave or very stupid.”

Elara’s expression didn’t change. “I prefer ‘informed.’”

Rin’s grin widened. “I like her. Can I keep her too?”
