# Part 6: The Taste of Power

NARRATOR:
“Killing students draws attention.”

“And you want to be invisible.” She stepped closer, her body blocking him from the view of the other students. “But you can’t be invisible forever, Emperor. Sooner or later, someone will force your hand. And when that happens, I want to be there to see what you become.”

Her hand brushed his arm, a gesture that looked casual but felt deliberate. Her mana tingled against his skin, warm and possessive.

“What do you want me to become?” he asked quietly.

She tilted her head, her golden eyes searching his face. “Free,” she said, and for a moment, he thought he saw something genuine beneath the mask. “Free enough to choose me. Not because you’re bound. Because you want to.”

Before he could respond, a scream cut through the training ground.

They turned. On the far side of the arena, a crowd was forming around one of the sparring rings. Students were shouting, instructors pushing through.

Kaelen moved without thinking, weaving through the crowd until he reached the front.

A Church cadet lay on the ground, his arm bent at an unnatural angle, blood pooling beneath him. Standing over him was a demon student—a girl with ram-like horns and a face twisted with fear.

“I didn’t mean to,” she was saying, her voice high and shaking. “He pushed me. He pushed me and I just—I reacted. I didn’t mean to—”
