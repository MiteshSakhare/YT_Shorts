# Part 4: The Glutton’s Appetite

NARRATOR:
Nodded.

Seraphina extended her hand across the table. “Agreed?”

Kaelen took it. Her grip was strong, her skin warm, and the moment their palms touched, the seal within him pulsed—not painfully this time, but with a deep, resonant hum, like a beast recognizing its master’s voice.

He met her golden eyes. “Agreed. For now.”

As they shook, the door to the dining chamber burst open. A demon student—breathless, uniform torn—stumbled in. “Lady Seraphina! The Church cadets… they’ve crossed the border. They’re attacking the demon quarter. They’re saying one of us is harboring a heretic. They’re saying…” His eyes darted to Kaelen. “…they’re saying it’s him.”

Kaelen released Seraphina’s hand and stood, straightening his uniform. The meek mask slid back into place, but behind his violet eyes, the Demon Emperor was already calculating.

“It seems,” he said quietly, “the game has begun whether we’re ready or not.”
