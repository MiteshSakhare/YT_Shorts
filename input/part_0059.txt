# Part 9: The Bonds That Bind

NARRATOR:
Seraphina was in the front row of the demon section, her expression composed, her hands folded in her lap. Beside her, Vorg and Lyss formed a wall of silent support. Rin had slipped into a seat near the back, her hair still wild, her grin firmly in place.

Elara sat in the human section, her notebook open, her dark eyes tracking Kaelen as he walked to the center of the hall.

The human judge rose, a scroll unrolling in her hands. "Kaelen Vane. After deliberation, the tribunal has reached the following verdict."

She read the words slowly, her voice echoing off the stone walls:

"On the charge of violating the Academy truce through a hostile display of mana: not guilty. The display was deemed a defensive reaction to external provocation, not an act of aggression."

A murmur rippled through the crowd. Kaelen saw Valerius's face contort with fury in the Church section.

"On the charge of heresy and mana corruption: the tribunal finds insufficient evidence to convict. However, the accused's mana remains classified as unusual and requires ongoing monitoring. To that end, the tribunal orders the following conditions."

The human judge's voice hardened. "First: Kaelen Vane will be restricted to Academy grounds for the duration of his enrollment. Second: He will submit to weekly mana evaluations by a neutral party appointed by the tribunal. Third: He will wear a limiter—a device that will prevent his mana from exceeding safe thresholds without his consent."
