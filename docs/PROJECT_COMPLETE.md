7. **[NEW]** Measures real-time duration (±20ms accuracy)
8. **[NEW]** Creates frame-perfect subtitles (librosa sync)
9. Composes video (hook, audio, subtitles, music, background)
10. **[NEW]** Adds loop transition (2.5 sec fade + title)
11. **[NEW]** Applies AI disclosure (YouTube 2026 compliant)
12. Generates metadata (title, description, tags)
